About Prof Bitange Ndemo

Prof Bitange Ndemo is a public servant currently serving as the Kenya’s Ambassador to the Kingdom of Belgium, Professor of Entrepreneurship, Technocrat, previously the Permanent Secretary of Kenya’s Ministry of Information and Communication, editor, advisor and a champion of Pan-Africanism.
