Our People

Meet the team 

Daniel Tanui

Deputy Head of Mission – Assists the Head of Mission in providing policy and strategic oversight on various matters of the Mission.

Deputy Head of Mission – Assists the Head of Mission in providing policy and strategic oversight on various matters of the Mission; including the EU-OACPS Partnership Agreement (Post Cotonou); the Kenya -Belgium Bilateral relations, economic and commercial engagements, representing the mission in various OACPS sub-committees and the Kenya – European Union (EU) relations. Previously worked at the Office of President and Provincial Administration before joining the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Amb.

 

Daniel Tanui served as Deputy Director -Peace and Security in the Ministry Headquarters and in the following of Kenya Missions abroad: Kenya High Commission Harare, Zimbabwe, Kenya Embassy- Stockholm, Kenya Embassy Brasilia, Brazil, Kenya Embassy Doha, Qatar and Kenya Embassy Khartoum, Sudan. Amb. Daniel Tanui has a wealth of experience in Foreign Policy and National Security Strategy. He is a graduate of the University of Nairobi- BA in Political Science, MA in International Studies (UON), Post -Graduate Diploma in Diplomacy and International Relations (UON), Executive Masters in Energy and Resources- Hamad Bin Khalifa University-Qatar and is an alumni of the National Defence College of Kenya
